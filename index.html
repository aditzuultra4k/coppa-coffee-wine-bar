About Coppa

A place for coffee, wine and good conversations.

Coppa brings together the comfort of a boutique cafe and the elegance of a wine bar. Whether guests come for a morning espresso, a relaxed meeting, an after-work glass of wine or a casual evening with friends, Coppa offers a warm atmosphere, curated drinks and attentive service.
